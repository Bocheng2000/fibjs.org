const Integer test::BDD = 0

Definition of BDD (Behavior Drive Development) test environment that supports describe, xdescribe, it, xit, before, after, beforeEach and afterEach, while initializing assert and expect.

const Integer test::TDD = 1

Definition of TDD (Test Drive Development) test environment to support suite, xsuite, test, xtest, setup, teardown, suiteSetup and suiteTeardown, while initializing assert and expect.
